Comparatives help us compare two things. Here are two types:

1. Comparatives of Superiority

These express when something is "more" than another thing.

  • Example: "My brother is taller than me."
  • Example: "He is richer than me."

2. Comparatives of Inferiority

These express when something is "less" than another thing.

  • Example: "She can't run as fast as me."
  • Example: "The teacher is less patient than us."
